Cookie Al Mode

Signature Chocalate Chip recipe with a scoop of vanilla ice cream

Cookie à la mode is a delightful dessert that combines the warmth of a freshly baked cookie with the cool, creamy indulgence of ice cream. Typically served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream atop a gooey chocolate chip cookie, this treat creates a perfect balance of textures and flavors—warm meets cold, chewy meets smooth, and sweet meets sweeter. It’s a simple yet luxurious experience that elevates the classic cookie into a decadent dessert, often garnished with chocolate drizzle, caramel, or a sprinkle of nuts for added flair.
