Lazagna

Our handmade noodles layyered with our famous tomato sauce, becheml and ground beef

Lasagna, one of the oldest pasta dishes, has its roots in ancient Rome, where a dish called "lasana" or "lasanum" referred to a cooking pot. The term evolved from the Greek word "laganon," which described flat sheets of pasta dough. The modern version of lasagna, with its layered pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, originated in Italy, particularly in the Emilia-Romagna region. Over centuries, it became a beloved dish worldwide, celebrated for its rich flavors and comforting layers.
