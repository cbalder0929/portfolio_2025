Chicken Sammich

Crispy golden brown fried chicken served on a brioche bun next to our crunchy home cut fries.

The chicken sandwich is a classic culinary creation that has earned its place as a favorite for its perfect combination of flavors and textures. At its core, it features juicy, tender chicken, whether crispy fried or perfectly grilled, nestled between two soft buns. The sandwich is endlessly versatile, allowing for creative toppings like lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese, or a range of flavorful sauces to enhance its appeal. Satisfying and convenient, the chicken sandwich strikes a balance between comfort and indulgence, making it a go-to meal for any occasion.
